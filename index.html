For the main filters selection you have to click on the button wich label describes the filter that you want to add in order to calculate the top 10 districts to live in NYC.

When a filter is activated you can modify the importance level for the final calculation of the districts score for the ranking.

Remember that the importance level means how important is to you an especific property for the ranking. IT DOESN'T MEAN the actual level for that property (for example far, medium and close distance for the closeness level).

Developer

Are you a NYU Stern School of Business student who can't find a nice place to live? a place that is close to the university , where you feel safe and last but not least affordable for a student. For all these people this website helps to take the right desition , you can filter all the districts in New York based on the principal filters below.

Closeness

Safety (Number of crimes)

Number of affordable units

And for these ones who have special interests for the place they want to live the website has another special options to make your search more personalized and take an even better desition.

Number of museums

Air quality

Number of art galleries

Number of farmers markets

You can control your search however you like by selecting the features for which you want to filter the districts. Also you have charts and graphics that help you to see the information clearly and a comparision tool that allows you to make a direct comparision of two district and see a summary of which of both districts is the best in each of the features. Finally you have all the information that you need to have of the districts and the rankings in tables, info windows, pop-ups, and more. So what are you waiting for? go and find your next home!
